About us

Travelling with MARILL

"Our passion is the bush"


Born and raised in the heart of Marill, Bowen and Ashley bring their deep-rooted love and connection to this unique land into every single tour they lead.

Their firsthand knowledge of the ... terrain, wildlife, and stories passed down through generations adds a personal and authentic touch that makes each journey with MARILL TOUR not just a trip, but a meaningful exploration.

Whether it's spotting rare and elusive wildlife in their natural habitat, watching the skies burst into vibrant colors at sunset, capturing breathtaking panoramic views, or delving into the rich cultural and historical tapestry of the Mallee region, your experience with MARILL TOUR is crafted to be both exciting and enriching.
